# Ender Sender 2.0.0 Changelog:

### Features

- Particle effects between sender and player when entering radius, communicating the player is linked to the sender's inventory.
- GUI based off default MC resources (so hopefully compatible with dark mode resource packs).
- Block replenishment as responsive as previous implementations, even at stack size of 1.
- gamerule to change radius of ender sender (in blocks, to a max of 64)
- gamerule to toggle the effects of ender sender (particles spawning at sender, player and in a line between them)

### Changes:

- Ported to fabric 1.20.1

### Notes:

- Has yet to be tested in multiplayer as of 2026-04-28.
